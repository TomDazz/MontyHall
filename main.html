Monty Hall Problem

The Monty Hall Problem is a probability puzzle based on a game show scenario. You are presented with three doors. Behind one of the doors is a car (the prize), and behind the other two doors are goats (undesirable outcomes). You choose one door. After your choice, the host, Monty Hall, who knows what is behind each door, opens another door revealing a goat. You are then given the option to either stick with your original choice or switch to the other unopened door. Counterintuitively, it is statistically better to always switch doors, as it gives you a higher chance of winning the car.
